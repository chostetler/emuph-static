Solution: Reassemblage
Answer: SEA SHANTY

Written by Hannah Leaman

In each pair of clues, you need to find two compound words that form another compound word when you put together the first part of the first one and the second part of the second one.

Pair Answer 1 Answer 2 Combined
1 AIRPLANE BABYPROOF AIRPROOF
2 SUPERSCRIPT ANTIHERO SUPERHERO
3 ANTLION FOOTHILL ANTHILL
4 IRONMAN CLOCKWORK IRONWORK
5 LIGHTWEIGHT COURTHOUSES LIGHTHOUSES
6 OUTSIDE HAIRDO OUTDO
7 RUNWAY TOUCHDOWN RUNDOWN
8 SHEEPSKIN CORNDOG SHEEPDOG

Reading the first letters of each combined word gives A SAILOR'S and reading the last letters gives FOLK SONG, which clues SEA SHANTY
